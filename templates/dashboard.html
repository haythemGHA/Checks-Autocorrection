Statistics about bank checks usage in Tunisia

• The cheque is a popular cashless payment instrument, used by both consumers and companies. Cheques account for half of all cashless payments in value terms.
• Cheques are truncated into electronic items before being settled via SIBTEL. Funds are available within 24 hours.
• In 2019, 26,159 thousand cheques were processed (26,123 thousand in 2018), with a value of TND 101,279.1 million (TND 93,380.1 million in 2018).

• 45% of the financial transactions in tunisia are done by bank checks, which shows that bank checks still have tunisians trust.
• The average number of checks used every year reaches 26 millions in 2020.
• 55% des montants payés dans le cadre des différentes transactions, durant les trois dernières années, ont été effectués par chèques avec un taux de rejet des chèques ne dépasse pas 2%

Number of checks by amount

The number of checks with a value under 20TND does not reach 180 thousand checks (representing a global value of 3 millions TND), however the checks with an amount between 100 TND and 20 TND, are in the range of 4 millions (a value of 300 millions TND)
